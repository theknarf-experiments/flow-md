# Connected notes

The classic Obsidian graph, as a Datalog view: nodes are notes, edges come
from the `Link(src, dst, kind)` relation the markdown plugin extracts from
`[[wiki links]]` and `[markdown](links)`. Click a node to open it; hover to
highlight its neighbourhood.

<Graph query='Link(src, dst, "wiki")' />

Because it's just a query, any derived relation works as the edge set. Pages
that share a tag:

```datalog
CoTagged(a, b, tag) :- Tag(a, tag), Tag(b, tag), a != b.
```

<Graph query="CoTagged(a, b, tag)" from="a" to="b" />
